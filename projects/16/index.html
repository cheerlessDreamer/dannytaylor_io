Autonomous Flight

I designed Britain's first autonomous passenger drone.

  • Brand
  • UX-UI
  • Industrial design

I lead the small team that designed Britain’s first passenger-carrying drone, and was responsible for all design output. Thi included the drone exterior and interior, as well as accompanying website and all presentations and investor material. I spearheaded the adoption of agile practises, and acted as the administrator for Jira. Additionally, I was responsible for recruitment and the company finances.

I worked closely with a flight engineer for almost a year to design Britain's first autonomous passenger-carrying drone for Autonomous Flight.

The drone has featured widely in the press, including within the Evening Standard, City AM, Sky News, The Times, the Evening Express, and BBC World News.

During this time, I was responsible for producing all design output, including the company website, promotional videos, and patent illustrations. To cope with the backlog, I pioneered the use of the Lean Agile methodology, and administered our JIRA and Confluence installations.
